## The four practices

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **Business Scenarios**

    ---

    The TOGAF Business Scenario technique re-expressed as a composable Essence practice. Discover and quantify the need before anything is committed.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Open practice](business-scenarios-essence-practice.html)

-   **Service Blueprint**

    ---

    Visualise the service delivery process from both the customer's and the provider's perspective — frontstage, backstage, and support processes.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Open practice](service-blueprint-essence-practice.html)

-   **Minimum Viable Architecture**

    ---

    Architect the minimum, just in time, to retire significant risks and guide delivery. Decisions over documents.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Open practice](mva-essence-practice.html)

-   **Minimum Viable Governance**

    ---

    Govern outcomes and guardrails, not gates. Decide at the last responsible moment, as close to the work as possible.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Open practice](mvg-essence-practice.html)

</div>
