    A Way of Working,
    composed with Essence

    This is the front door to a complete way of working built on the OMG / SEMAT Essence standard: a kernel of universal things to track, plus four composable practices that move them. This page explains Essence, then shows how the kernel and practices fit together — and the logical order in which they move the alphas.

    Essence is an Open Group / OMG standard developed by the SEMAT community (Ivar Jacobson and others). This way of working composes the Essence kernel with four practices — Business Scenarios and Service Blueprint (discovery & service design), Minimum Viable Architecture and Minimum Viable Governance — synthesising ideas from TOGAF, the TOGAF Business Scenarios method, service blueprinting (Shostack, 1984) and Use-Case 2.0. The composition and card form are a synthesis for learning; keep your licensed standards as the authority.
