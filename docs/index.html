Essence is an OMG standard developed by the SEMAT community (Ivar Jacobson and others). This way of working composes the kernel with four practices synthesising ideas from TOGAF, service blueprinting (Shostack, 1984) and Use-Case 2.0. Keep your licensed standards as the authority.
